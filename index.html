About Me

Originally from Germany, Sascha worked and studied in 3 different countries. Currently he resides in the sunshine state, in the city of Angeles. In 2011 Sascha finished his degree in Management Science from the University of California, San Diego. Then he started consulting several German companies on its processes before moving into the startup world. There he first lead the operations of an online marketing startup, which we all know means: Sascha did everything, from brewing coffee, cold calling, hiring to also defining the operations.

Eventually he was called to Dublin, to help Google build an agency focused brand team. Since November 2012, Google helps him build his career and skills. On the one side he advises and supports sales on Google’s Brand Products, mostly YouTube, and other side consults engineering to help advance Google’s proprietary brand tools.
